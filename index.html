QR Code Generator for Tools

Generate a QR code for use in device setup

Installing

  • Make sure your bookmarks toolbar or sidebar is visible (usually found under your browser's View menu).
  • Drag the button below onto your bookmarks toolbar or sidebar.

Using

After looking up a device's serial number in Tools, click this bookmarklet. A QR code will be generated that you can scan for use in device setups in the apps.
